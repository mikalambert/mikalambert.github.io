Art and Photography

When I need to de-stress from a long week, I gravitate towards painting with either watercolor or gouache. The process is relaxing and allows me to "flow". I've had a couple of paintings showcased in small art galleries and being surround by so many amazing artists is truly awe-inspiring!

Astrophotography is a completely different hobby that is much more methodical and requires a lot more planning. Post-processing the images is the most exciting part for me because the final result can change drastically depending on how the process goes!

Hiking

I've always enjoyed exploring the outdoors, and the best way to fit that into my lifestyle now is through hiking!

Some of my favorite places I've hiked are Chiricahua National Monument, Mt. Lemmon, and Picacho peak in Arizona, as well as the Rocky Mountains in Colorado!

Santa Cruz is one of the most diverse places in California, with towering redwoods and dramatic cliffs overlooking the ocean. I've begun exploring the area and my favorite places are Wilder Ranch State Beach and Henry Cowell State Park.
